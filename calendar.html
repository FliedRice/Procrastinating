Calendar

A life Calendar filled with boxes. every box is worth 1 week of your life and each row of weeks makes up one year. 90 columns to be filled ,90 years to fill, a calendar of a lifetime. 90 years of boxes to fill in seems alot, but most of us have already filled in quite alot of those boxes

Yet you still are wasting yout time away, for how long shall you continue on the path of nothingness. Get up and set your goals, plans and deadlines, and go to work on them today. There are only about 90 years you can enjoy. Everyday you keep procrastinating on every little thing, while saying to yourself:"I'll Leave Tomorrow's Problems To Tomorrow's Me".

That is why we will start by filling in these boxes with something meaningfull every week, or maybe next week......
