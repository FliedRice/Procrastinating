About Procrastination station

we are all about avoiding procrastination. So elegant in its simplicity, Yet so hard to actualy do.

but,“avoid procrastination” is only good advice for fake procrastinators—those people that are like, “I totally go on Facebook a few times every day at work—I’m such a procrastinator!” The same people that will say to a real procrastinator something like, “Just don’t procrastinate and you’ll be fine.”

The thing that neither the dictionary nor fake procrastinators understand is that for a real procrastinator, procrastination isn’t optional—it’s something they don’t know how to not do. Thats is why they can't just stop.

That's why Procrastination Station have come here with a handy site that can help you get things done. This won't cure any procrastinator, but will aid them in the journey of overcoming it.
